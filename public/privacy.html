Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Air-Care Connect LLC ("Air-Care Connect," "Aire Azul," "we," "us") provides HVAC installation, repair, maintenance, and duct cleaning services in Central Florida. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you request a quote, book a service call, or communicate with us.

Information we collect

When you request an estimate or book a service call, we collect your name, address, phone number, email address, and a description of the service you need. We use this information solely to schedule, perform, and follow up on your service appointment.

How we use your information

We use your contact information to confirm appointments, send updates if a scheduled time changes, and communicate with you about the service you requested. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Text message communications

If you provide a phone number, you consent to receive text messages from Air-Care Connect related to your service appointment, including scheduling confirmations and updates if your appointment time changes. Message frequency varies based on your appointment activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out of text messages, or HELP for assistance.

Data retention

We retain appointment and contact information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide our services and maintain business records.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be directed to Air-Care Connect at the contact information listed on air-careconnect.com.
